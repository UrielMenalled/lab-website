Teaching

Cover Crops in Agroecosystems (PLSCI 4125/6125)

Role: Lecturer

Institution: Cornell University

Term: Fall 2023

This course explores the role of cover crops in sustainable agricultural systems, examining their ecological functions, management practices, and integration into diverse cropping systems.

Teaching Philosophy

Our approach to teaching emphasizes experiential learning, critical thinking, and the integration of ecological principles with practical agricultural applications. We believe that effective agricultural education must connect scientific concepts with real-world farming challenges and opportunities.

Through hands-on activities, field experiences, and collaborative projects, students develop both the technical knowledge and the practical skills needed to contribute to sustainable agriculture.

Our lab conducts research on sustainable agriculture, weed ecology, cover crops, and agroecological systems. Below is a selection of recent peer-reviewed publications.

Recent Publications

Our research has been published in leading journals including npj Sustainable Agriculture, Weed Science, Ecological Applications, Scientific Reports, and Field Crops Research.

Key research areas include:

  • Legacy effects of crop diversity on weed-crop competition
  • Cover crop mixtures and their role in organic no-till systems
  • Phylogenetic relatedness in cover crop-based weed suppression
  • Multi-tactic weed management strategies
  • Roles of crop diversification in forage systems
  • Cereal rye mulch biomass effects on weed communities
  • Soil-mediated effects on weed-crop competition
  • Plant-soil feedbacks in different farming systems

For a complete list of publications with full citations, please visit our Google Scholar profile.

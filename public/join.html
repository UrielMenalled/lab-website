Lab Philosophy

What We Expect From You

  • Collaboration: Be a valued team member and share your unique perspectives
  • Eagerness to learn: Set goals for your experience and communicate your aspirations
  • Self-advocacy: Work independently but know when to ask for help and support
  • Preparation: Be ready to meet deadlines, especially during fieldwork season
  • Honesty: Communicate openly about what's working and speak up about mistakes
  • Flexibility: Embrace the unexpected challenges that come with scientific research
  • Respect: Value diverse backgrounds and perspectives within the research community

What You Can Expect From Us

  • Respect: A welcoming environment for people of all backgrounds and identities
  • Open-mindedness: Your opinions will be valued and taken seriously
  • Availability: Regular meetings to discuss progress, questions, and findings
  • Professional development: Opportunities for conferences, teaching, and on-farm research
  • Advocacy: Support in building skills and confidence for your chosen career path
  • Long-term commitment: Ongoing support beyond graduation for future career endeavors

Our Mentorship Approach

We believe in fostering independent researchers who can think critically and work collaboratively. Our mentorship style emphasizes mutual respect, open communication, and professional growth tailored to each student's goals and aspirations.

Whether you're interested in pursuing an academic career, working in agricultural extension, or contributing to sustainable farming practices, we're committed to helping you develop the skills and network you need to succeed.
